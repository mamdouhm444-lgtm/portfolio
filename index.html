04 — Proactive Curation

The 4-Product Formula

Invite clients into the magical world of Jimmy Choo

As artists of joy, we proactively propose 2–3 additional items using this curated framework to elevate every client interaction.

◆
The Requested
The exact product requested by the client—the foundation of the interaction.
◆
The Extra
A supporting item meeting the same need—alternative size, heel height, or colorway.
◆
The Alternative
A partnering accessory that captures their imagination—bag, scarf, eyewear, fragrance, SLG, or belt.
◆
The Dream
A high-ticket or new collection piece curated from lifestyle discovery that you know they will love.
